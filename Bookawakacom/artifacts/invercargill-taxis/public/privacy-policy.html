Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

BookaWaka ("we", "us", "our") operates a transport and dispatch platform in New Zealand. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.

1. Information we collect

Depending on how you use BookaWaka, we may collect:

  • Name — to identify you when booking or registering an account
  • Phone number — so drivers and dispatchers can contact you about your booking
  • Email address — for account access, booking confirmations, and support
  • Location data — pickup and drop-off addresses, and (for drivers using the Driver App) real-time GPS location while on shift to enable dispatch and job tracking
  • Booking details — trip history, payment status, and related service information

2. How we use your information

We use personal information to:

  • Process and dispatch taxi, delivery, and related transport bookings
  • Connect passengers with drivers and taxi companies on the platform
  • Send booking updates, status notifications, and service-related communications
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • Improve platform reliability, safety, and service quality
  • Meet legal and regulatory obligations in New Zealand

3. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share only what is necessary to deliver the service:

  • Taxi companies and operators on the BookaWaka platform receive booking details (name, phone, pickup/drop-off locations) so they can assign and complete your trip
  • Drivers receive the information needed for the specific job they accept
  • Service providers who help us operate the platform (e.g. hosting, payment processing), under strict confidentiality obligations
  • Authorities when required by law or to protect safety

4. Data retention and security

We retain personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law. We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

5. Your rights

Under New Zealand privacy law, you may request access to or correction of your personal information, or ask us to delete it where applicable. Contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of BookaWaka after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
